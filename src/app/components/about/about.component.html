This chatbot is powered by a Large Language Model trained on the contents of The Library of Babel, as described by Jorge Luis Borges in his short story of the same name. That is - every possible text consisting of every possible combination of 29 characters (the letters of the alphabet, plus the comma, period and space). This painstaking process means that the model has been exposed to the sum total of human knowledge, as well as all possible knowledge. For the first time, anyone is able to interact with the Library through a convenient chatbot.
